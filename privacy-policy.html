​Privacy Policy

​1. Info We Grab

When you use our site, we might grab some info about you, both personal and not.

a. Personal Stuff
This is info you give us on purpose, like:

Your name, email, phone number, and address.

If you buy something, your payment info.

Anything else you type in when you contact us, sign up, or fill out a form.

b. Non-Personal Stuff
We might automatically grab stuff like:

Your IP address, browser type, what kind of computer you’re using.

What pages you look at, how long you stay on our site, and how you click around.

Cookies and other tracking stuff.

c. Super-Secret Stuff
We don’t try to grab super-secret personal info (like health stuff or social security numbers) unless you tell us to.

2. How We Use Your Info

We might use your info to:

Keep our site and services running and make them better.

Answer your questions or help you out.

Send you newsletters or updates (if you say it’s okay).

Make the site easier and better for you.

See what’s popular, how people use the site, and where they click.

Do what we have to by law and make sure everyone follows the rules.

3. Sharing Your Info

We respect your privacy. We don’t sell or rent your personal info. But we might share it if:

We hire other companies to help us with stuff (like hosting the site, looking at how people use it, or sending emails).

The law tells us we have to.

We sell the company or merge with another company.

We have to protect our rights, our stuff, or keep people safe.

4. Cookies

We use cookies and other similar things to:

Make the site work better for you.

See how people use the site.

Show you stuff you might like.

See if our ads are working.

You can turn off cookies in your browser, but some parts of the site might not work right.

5. Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you might be able to:

See the personal info we have about you.

Ask us to fix it, delete it, or stop using it.

Tell us to stop sending you emails.

Say you don’t want us to use your info in certain ways.

6. How We Keep Your Info Safe

We try our best to keep your info safe, but no system is perfect. You’re taking a chance any time you send info online.

7. How Long We Keep Your Info

We only keep your personal info long enough to:

Give you the services you asked for.

Do what the law says we have to.

Deal with problems or make sure everyone follows the rules.

After that, we’ll either delete your info or make it anonymous.

8. Links to Other Sites

Our site might have links to other websites. We’re not responsible for what those sites do. Check their privacy policies before you give them any info.

9. Kid’s Privacy

Our site is not for kids under 13. We don’t try to grab info from kids. If we find out we have it, we’ll delete it ASAP.

10. If You’re Outside the [your country]

If you’re using our site from another country, your info might be sent to countries with different privacy laws. By using our site, you’re okay with that.

11. Changes to This Policy

We might change this privacy policy from time to time. When we do, we’ll post the new policy on this page with a new date. Check back here every so often.
